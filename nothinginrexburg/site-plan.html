Overview

Purpose

Think about the last time you and your friends were trying to decide what to do for a weekend here in Rexburg. Hard to figure what there is to do right? Well, I aim to solve that problem. How do you feel when you are trying to figure that out? Maybe a little frustrated or a little annoyed, well my website will give you a stress free way to figure out what to do. Taking posts, blogs and knowledge from locals, my website will have all the latest hits in Rexburg to finally put to bed the common phrase "There's nothing to do in Rexburg!"

Audience

My intended audience is anyone looking for something to do on a weekend/weekday in Rexburg. Though it can be virtually anyone the obvious audience here is the students of BYU-I who will be looking for things to do that may not know about much to do.

Home page

Top 3 Recommendations for things to do in Rexburg. 1. Roots and Boots: The Ultimate Country Dancing Hot Spot Looking for a night of fun, dancing, and new friends? Roots and Boots is the place to be! This local country dancing hot spot is open on Wednesdays, Fridays, and Saturdays. With an entry fee of just $5, you'll get a fantastic evening starting with dance instruction from 7:30 to 8 PM, followed by dancing until midnight. The live DJ keeps the energy high with great music, and there's always plenty of people to dance with, whether you're a seasoned dancer or just starting out. The atmosphere is lively and welcoming, making it a must-visit for anyone looking to experience the local culture and have a blast. 2. Righteous Slice: The Best Pizza in Town If you're a pizza lover, Righteous Slice is an absolute must-visit in Rexburg. Known for having the best pizza in town, this place uses amazing wood ovens that cook the pizzas to perfection. While it's on the pricier side, the quality and taste are well worth it. With a variety of unique pizzas to choose from, or the option to build your own, there's something for everyone. Each pizza is large enough to feed two very hungry college students, making it a perfect spot for a casual yet delicious meal. The atmosphere is cozy and inviting, ideal for a date night or a fun dinner with friends. 3. Porter Park: A Classic Outdoor Retreat For a relaxing day out in the spring or summer, Porter Park is Rexburg's go-to spot. This beautiful park offers plenty of space to play sports on the lush grass, with courts available for basketball, tennis, and pickleball. It's a fantastic place for a BBQ or picnic, with numerous picnic areas shaded by lovely trees. Whether you're looking to enjoy some outdoor activities, have a leisurely meal with family and friends, or simply soak up the sun, Porter Park provides a perfect setting. The park's vibrant and welcoming atmosphere makes it a staple in the community and a wonderful place to unwind.

Food & Treats

The Hickory: The Hickory in Rexburg, Idaho offers a mouthwatering array of BBQ delights and more. Their menu features tender smoked meats, juicy burgers, and flavorful sides that will satisfy any craving. Don’t miss their signature ribs, perfectly seasoned and cooked to fall-off-the-bone perfection. Pair your meal with their refreshing drink options, including local craft beers. The atmosphere is warm and welcoming, perfect for family gatherings or casual meals with friends. Prices are reasonable, offering great value for the quality and portions. Whether you're a local or just passing through, The Hickory is a must-visit for an unforgettable dining experience. For more details, visit The Hickory. Pick Me Up Drinks: If you're in Rexburg and craving something special, Pick Me Up Drinks is the place to be. This vibrant soda shop offers a wide range of delicious mixed drinks, perfect for lifting your spirits any day. Imagine sipping on creative concoctions like the “My Little Pony” while enjoying a friendly, uplifting atmosphere. Their cookies are a delightful treat too! The vibe here is all about spreading cheer and making connections, making every visit feel like a small celebration. Whether you’re grabbing a drink to-go or hanging out with friends, Pick Me Up Drinks ensures you leave with a smile. Check out their full menu and more details on their website. Original Thai: If you're in Rexburg, Idaho, and looking for a fantastic locally-owned spot with rave reviews, you should definitely check out Original Thai. As someone who loves exploring hidden gems, I can tell you this place is a must-visit for any Thai food enthusiast. Original Thai offers a delightful array of flavorful dishes that are sure to tantalize your taste buds. Whether you're in the mood for their signature Drunken Noodles, the rich and creamy Pineapple Curry, or the crispy and savory Crab Rangoon, each dish is crafted with authentic Thai flavors that truly stand out. The ambiance at Original Thai is warm and inviting, featuring cozy, cultural touches like hammered metal tables and hanging paper lanterns. It creates a unique dining experience that feels both intimate and vibrant. The prices are reasonable, making it a great option for both lunch and dinner, and the service is consistently quick and friendly. This combination of delicious food, welcoming atmosphere, and excellent service makes Original Thai a standout choice in Rexburg. Whether you're grabbing a quick meal or enjoying a leisurely dinner, you won't be disappointed!

Events

Beehive Credit Union Picnic: If you're looking for a fun, family-friendly event in Rexburg, Idaho, you won't want to miss the Beehive Federal Credit Union's Annual Summer Picnic. Held at the picturesque Porter Park, this picnic is a lively celebration that brings the community together for an afternoon of entertainment, delicious food, and engaging activities. Taking place on June 23 from 4 PM to 7 PM, the picnic offers a variety of entertainment options that cater to all ages. You can enjoy live cowboy singing from The Teton Riders and entertaining poetry from Andy Nelson, creating a festive western atmosphere. Dress up in your best cowboy gear for a chance to win awards for the best cowboy hat, belt buckle, bolo tie, and boots. The event features a wide array of activities, including a waterslide, carousel, bounce houses, human foosball, face painting, a fish pond, and a climbing wall. For just $5, you can get a wristband that grants access to all these games and activities (free for children under 2). Plus, don't miss the chance to try the mechanical bull ride, which adds to the cowboy-themed fun. In terms of food, Beehive members can enjoy a free meal that includes hot dogs, chips, salad, lemonade, and an ice cream bar. Non-members can also join in for $8. Additionally, you can purchase cotton candy and shaved ice for just $1 each. One of the best parts of this event is that all proceeds go to Primary Children's Hospital, making it a fun day out for a great cause Whoopee Days Rodeo: If you're looking for an exciting event, the Whoopee Days Rodeo at the Madison County Fairgrounds is a must-visit! Held from June 27th to 29th, 2024, the rodeo offers a thrilling experience with gates opening at 5:00 PM. Each night has a unique theme: Family Night on Thursday with affordable ticket bundles, Cancer Awareness Night on Friday featuring a raffle, and Patriotic Day on Saturday capped off with a spectacular fireworks show. Mutton Bustin' for kids adds extra fun, and the energetic atmosphere promises a memorable time for all. For more details, visit the Whoopee Days Rodeo page. 4th of July: If you're looking for a fun-filled way to celebrate Independence Day, Rexburg has an exciting lineup for you! The Fourth of July festivities kick off with a lively parade through downtown, showcasing local bands, floats, and performers. The celebration continues at Porter Park with games, food vendors, and live music, creating a vibrant and community-focused atmosphere. The highlight of the evening is the spectacular fireworks show that lights up the sky, providing a perfect end to a day full of patriotic fun. Don’t miss out on this all-day event that brings the community together in celebration! For more details, visit the Fourth of July Page.
