Welcome

Think about the last time you and your friends were trying to decide what to do for a weekend here in Rexburg. Hard to figure what there is to do right? Well, I aim to solve that problem. How do you feel when you are trying to figure that out? Maybe a little frustrated or a little annoyed, well my website will give you a stress free way to figure out what to do. Taking posts, blogs and knowledge from locals, my website will have all the latest hits in Rexburg to finally put to bed the common phrase "There's nothing to do in Rexburg!"

Top 3 Recommendations for things to do in Rexburg

Roots & Boots

The Ultimate Country Dancing Hot Spot. Looking for a night of fun, dancing, and new friends? Roots and Boots is the place to be! This local country dancing hot spot is open on Wednesdays, Fridays, and Saturdays. With an entry fee of just $5, you'll get a fantastic evening starting with dance instruction from 7:30 to 8 PM, followed by dancing until midnight. The live DJ keeps the energy high with great music, and there's always plenty of people to dance with, whether you're a seasoned dancer or just starting out. The atmosphere is lively and welcoming, making it a must-visit for anyone looking to experience the local culture and have a blast.

Righteous Slice

The Best Pizza in Town. If you're a pizza lover, Righteous Slice is an absolute must-visit in Rexburg. Known for having the best pizza in town, this place uses amazing wood ovens that cook the pizzas to perfection. While it's on the pricier side, the quality and taste are well worth it. With a variety of unique pizzas to choose from, or the option to build your own, there's something for everyone. Each pizza is large enough to feed two very hungry college students, making it a perfect spot for a casual yet delicious meal. The atmosphere is cozy and inviting, ideal for a date night or a fun dinner with friends.

Porter Park

A Classic Outdoor Retreat. For a relaxing day out in the spring or summer, Porter Park is Rexburg's go-to spot. This beautiful park offers plenty of space to play sports on the lush grass, with courts available for basketball, tennis, and pickleball. It's a fantastic place for a BBQ or picnic, with numerous picnic areas shaded by lovely trees. Whether you're looking to enjoy some outdoor activities, have a leisurely meal with family and friends, or simply soak up the sun, Porter Park provides a perfect setting. The park's vibrant and welcoming atmosphere makes it a staple in the community and a wonderful place to unwind.
