Food & Treats!

The Hickory

The Hickory in Rexburg, Idaho offers a mouthwatering array of BBQ delights and more. Their menu features tender smoked meats, juicy burgers, and flavorful sides that will satisfy any craving. Don’t miss their signature ribs, perfectly seasoned and cooked to fall-off-the-bone perfection. Pair your meal with their refreshing drink options, including local craft beers. The atmosphere is warm and welcoming, perfect for family gatherings or casual meals with friends. Prices are reasonable, offering great value for the quality and portions. Whether you're a local or just passing through, The Hickory is a must-visit for an unforgettable dining experience. For more details, visit The Hickory.

Pick Me Up Drinks

If you're in Rexburg and craving something special, Pick Me Up Drinks is the place to be. This vibrant soda shop offers a wide range of delicious mixed drinks, perfect for lifting your spirits any day. Imagine sipping on creative concoctions like the “My Little Pony” while enjoying a friendly, uplifting atmosphere. Their cookies are a delightful treat too! The vibe here is all about spreading cheer and making connections, making every visit feel like a small celebration. Whether you're grabbing a drink to-go or hanging out with friends, Pick Me Up Drinks ensures you leave with a smile. Check out their full menu and more details on their website.

Original Thai

If you're in Rexburg, Idaho, and looking for a fantastic locally-owned spot with rave reviews, you should definitely check out Original Thai. As someone who loves exploring hidden gems, I can tell you this place is a must-visit for any Thai food enthusiast. Original Thai offers a delightful array of flavorful dishes that are sure to tantalize your taste buds. Whether you're in the mood for their signature Drunken Noodles, the rich and creamy Pineapple Curry, or the crispy and savory Crab Rangoon, each dish is crafted with authentic Thai flavors that truly stand out. The ambiance at Original Thai is warm and inviting, featuring cozy, cultural touches like hammered metal tables and hanging paper lanterns. It creates a unique dining experience that feels both intimate and vibrant. The prices are reasonable, making it a great option for both lunch and dinner, and the service is consistently quick and friendly. This combination of delicious food, welcoming atmosphere, and excellent service makes Original Thai a standout choice in Rexburg. Whether you're grabbing a quick meal or enjoying a leisurely dinner, you won't be disappointed!
